Pittsburgh AR Wayfinding App

**Download only available for iPhone/iPad running iOS 11.4 or later, please update your operating system if required**

Please read all instructions before downloading the PGH AR App.

IMPORTANT! The app will download to the last page on your devices screens. After the app is installed on your device you need to trust the app's author before it will run. The steps to do this are as follows:

1) On your device go to Settings > General > (Profiles &) Device Management

2) In (Profiles &) Device Management look for the "Enterprise App" profile and click on the one titled "Arup"

3) Hit "Trust "Arup"" and then hit "Trust" on the popup screen that follows

4) The PGH AR app should now be ready to use on your device. Enjoy!
