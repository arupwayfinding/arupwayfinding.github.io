Pittsburgh AR Wayfinding App hosting page

**Download only available for iPhone running iOS 11.0 or later, please update your operating system if required**

Please read all instructions before downloading the PGH AR App

Once the app is downloaded to your phone you need to trust the author of the app, the steps to do this are as follows:

1) On your phone go to Settings > General > Profiles & Device Management

2) In Profiles & Device Management look for the "Enterprise App" profile and click on the one called "Arup"

3) Hit Trust "App"

4) The PGH AR app should now be ready to use on your device. Enjoy!
